Our Move In & Move Out Checklist

Please Note: Our Move In/Move Out option delivers a thorough cleaning to prepare the home for you or the new family moving in. Please ensure that the home is empty prior to our cleaning and that there are no other people (movers, tradespeople etc) working in the space. Please ensure that electricity and water are turned on prior to your scheduled cleaning, as both are required for us to complete the service.

Additionally, if the home is particularly dirty, we may need to add the First Time Clean service to your cleaning in order to give our cleaners time to complete their work.

  • Baseboards and window sills dusted
